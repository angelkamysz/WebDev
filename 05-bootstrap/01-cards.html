Chrome Dino Game

The Chrome Dino Game was released in 2014 by Google. It appears when users lose internet connection in Chrome.

Players control a dinosaur that jumps over obstacles and avoids enemies. It’s simple, fun, and addictive.

Untitled Goose Game

Untitled Goose Game was released in 2019 by House House. It is a puzzle-stealth game where players control a mischievous goose.

The game received praise for its humor, unique concept, and charming design.

Flappy Bird

Flappy Bird was released in 2013 by Dong Nguyen. It is a mobile game where players tap the screen to keep a bird flying.

The game became extremely popular because of its difficulty and simple design.
